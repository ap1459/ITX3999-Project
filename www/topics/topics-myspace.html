MySpace was launched in 2003 and spearheaded by Tom Anderson and Chris DeWolfe. It enjoyed huge popularity between the years of 2004 to 2009, and was the largest social network at that time! It even surpassed Google as the most visited site in the USA in 2006! Unfortunately for them, in 2009 they were surpassed by Facebook in the number of unique visitors, and slowly became less and less popular. Despite this, the site still gets 50.6 million unique visists a month.

Key Safety Points
  • Information - You don't want to give away too much or unecessary information about yourself, only the bare minimum, as someone with malicious intent may use this against you
  • Anonymity - Just because you may have decided to not use your realy name, it does not mean you are anonymous! If are going to post pictures of yourself online, you must understand they are online indefinitely!
  • Friends - Only accept the friend requests of people you know! You never know if someone online is who they actually say they are
