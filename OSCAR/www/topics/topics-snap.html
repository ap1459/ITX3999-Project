Snapchat was initially released back in 2011, created by Evan Spiegel, Reggie Brown and Bobby Murphy What makes this service stand out from other messaging services, is that when a user sends a picture or video another, they are only available to be viewed for a very short amount of time, after which they can no longer be accessed and are discarded. Users can also update their "Story", which lasts for 24 hours and may be a video or image, and this is a medium by which they can share content with all their contacts at the same time. It currently enjoys a daily active user base of 187 million people!

Key Safety Points
  • Sometimes Snapchat will recommed mutual friends to you, these should only be accepted if you know the person!
  • Don't send anything innapropriate in a snap! You may trust your friends, but take into account that anyone could potentailly see the snap once it has been opened
  • Only snap images/video to people you trust, as they may very well decide to screenshot it, even though Snapchat warns you this has happened, there isn't anything you can really do about it
  • Never tweet any personal or identifable information!
  • Beware of bit.ly and http://t.co links! These may be tweeted by someone, or sent to you in a direct message. These are shorthand links and you shouldn't click them unless you can verify you were sent them legitametly, and not by a hacker!
